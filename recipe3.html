MASAL DOSA

Masala Dosa is a classic South Indian dish that consists of a crispy crepe made from a fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato mixture. Here's a simplified recipe to help you make Masala Dosa at home.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 to 5 medium potatoes, boiled and chopped
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal, soaked
  • A pinch of asafoetida(hing)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 green cjilli, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons broken cashew nuts(optional)
  • A pinch of turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil
  • 2-3 tablespoons finely chopped coriander leaves

PROCEDURE:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them crackle.
  2. Add asadoetida, soaked chana dal, cumin seeds, and urad dal(if using). Saute until they turn light brown.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilli, and cashew nuts.Saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Add onions and cook until translucent.
  5. Add turmeric powder, salt, and a little water. Cook until the water starts boiling.
  6. Add the chopped potatoes, mix well, and lightly mash with a spoon. Finish with coriander leaves.

TIPS:

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your homemade masala dosa!. Happy Cooking!
